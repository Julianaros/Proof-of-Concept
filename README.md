# Proof-of-Concept

Develop a baseline technique and validate it by simulation (preferably using Qiskit) which shows that adding layers of Heisenberg gates (search yourself online) to the ansatz circuit of my paper PROGRESSIVELY
(1) bring the statevector closer to Quantum Spin Liquid state, without Hamiltonian calibration and 
(2) Achieves benchmark ground-state energy with little to none  Hamiltonian calibration.'  

You can start with two-unit cells (19-site lattice as shown in the paper supp. Material), and then try scale it up. Not an easy problem, because, despite years of research and stuff, no-one has been able to prepare QSL in a scalable manner, while achieving benchmark energy.
